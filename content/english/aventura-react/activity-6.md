### What comes next?

Now that you know the fundamentals of React, you can keep exploring:

- **Add a new stop to the tour:** Create a new component for another place in Costa Rica (for example, Manuel Antonio National Park or Río Celeste).
- **Improve the design:** Experiment with new colors, animations, or images in your `style.css`.
- **Learn more React:** Visit the [official React documentation](https://react.dev/learn) to discover concepts like `useEffect`, page navigation, and how to publish your app on the internet.
- **Share your work:** Show your app to your friends and family. You are a React programmer!
